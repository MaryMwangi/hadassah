About Us
Welcome to Hadassah: Your One Stop Shop for Fruits, Herbs and Seedlings.
At Hadassah, we believe in the power of nature to transform lives. Our mission is to provide high-quality fruits, herbs, seedlings, and plants to help you create a thriving garden. Whether you’re a seasoned gardener or just starting out, we have everything you need to grow a lush, productive garden..
Why Choose Us

  • Quality Guaranteed: We source our products from trusted growers to ensure you receive the healthiest and most robust plants.
  • Expert Advice: Our team of gardening experts is here to help you every step of the way. From planting tips to pest control, we’ve got you covered.
  • Sustainable Practices: We are committed to sustainable gardening practices that protect the environment and promote biodiversity.
